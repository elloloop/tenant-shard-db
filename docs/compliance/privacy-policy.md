## 8. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures consistent with
industry standards and applicable law, including:

- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256 via KMS).
- Principal-based access control (EntDB ACL v2) with least privilege.
- Immutable audit logging of administrative and data-plane operations.
- Annual penetration testing and vulnerability scanning.
- Multi-AZ deployment with documented RTO/RPO targets.
- Annual security awareness training for all staff.

For a complete inventory of controls, see our SOC 2 report and ISO
27001 Statement of Applicability under NDA.
